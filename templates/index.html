Diet & Nutrition

Healthy Eating Habits

A diabetic patient needs to be careful with the foods they eat. Maintaining a balanced diet is one of the most effective ways to manage blood sugar levels and prevent complications. Focus on nutrient-rich foods, monitor carbohydrate intake, and work with our specialists to create a meal plan that fits your lifestyle.
